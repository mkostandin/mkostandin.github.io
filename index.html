So, What the FPGA?

Matt Kostandin + Electrical Engineer

FPGA Oscilloscope

I love FPGAs! I am crazy about them. They are not only fun to make projects with but so powerful that they can play an important role on my workbench. I designed this practical usb oscilloscope to be affordable and easily reconfigurable for whatever I might need.

Learn more

Industrial Imaging

After gaining a year of experience as an engineer, I identified projects I wanted to use to demonstrate to my team why we would want to consider adding a FPGA to our inventory. This is one of them. It uses a MiPi sensor and a FPGA to identify defects in a part.

Learn more

Vitis AI Embedded Application

Having an integrated processor on board with the FPGA like this Kria SOM does opens the door to a whole slew of embedded applications. Here is one where I configured the FPGA to use facial recognition using the Vitis AI framework and an ordinary usb camera.

Learn more

Additional Projects

I am passionate about FPGAs. They are beautifully versatile devices. But they are not my only interest. I also love software engineering, web development, pcb design, and microcontrollers.
